Why You Need Certified Removal Experts

Attempting to fell a large tree without proper training and equipment is incredibly dangerous and can lead to severe property damage. In the Eugene and Springfield area, trees often grow perilously close to houses, power lines, and neighboring properties.

We connect you with certified arborists who utilize state-of-the-art rigging techniques and crane assistance to safely dismantle trees piece-by-piece, ensuring zero impact to your surrounding landscape.

Our Strict Vetting Guarantee

Every professional in our network is verified for active CCB licensing, comprehensive insurance coverage, and 4.5+ star community review ratings before we connect them to your project.

Signs You Need Tree Removal:

  • Dead or dying branches at the canopy crown.
  • Visible root decay or sudden leaning.
  • Severe storm damage or lightning strikes.
  • Proximity to new construction or power lines.
