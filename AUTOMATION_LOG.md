## 🏗️ Automation Architecture (Scalability Template)
This project is built to seamlessly scale to other cities and niches (e.g., West Seattle Hardscapes). 
- **Frontend:** Vanilla HTML/CSS with JavaScript form handling.
- **Backend:** Netlify Functions (Serverless code, Zero monthly hosting fees).
- **Communication:** Transitioning from Twilio (paid) to Webhooks (Discord/Telegram) and Free SMTP to ensure maximum profit margin.

## 💰 Monetization Roadmap (Eugene Tree Niche)

1. **Lead Generation (Current Phase)**
   - Optimize the site for Eugene/Springfield local SEO to generate organic traffic.
   - Capture leads securely using the custom built Netlify form.
2. **Contractor Vetting**
   - Verify Eugene-based contractors against the CCB (Construction Contractors Board) and ensure 4.5+ star ratings.
3. **Simul-Ring / Lead Distribution**
   - Distribute the incoming lead instantly to 3-5 vetted contractors.
   - The first contractor to claim the lead wins it.
4. **Data Leasing (Future Phase)**
   - Once a consistent volume of leads (e.g., 20+ per month) is achieved, lease the entire website exclusively to one high-performing contractor for a flat monthly fee (Rank and Rent model).
